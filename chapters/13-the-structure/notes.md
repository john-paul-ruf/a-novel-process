# Chapter 13 — Author Notes

**Voice Decisions:**
Cole's POV returns after Ren's chapter with deeper interiority than before. The confidence that builds through the first three beats — the elegant system, the branching network, the circulatory logic — is rendered in Cole's natural mode: precise, structural, mapping the problem toward a solution. The collapse in Beat 4 breaks that mode deliberately. The prose shifts from analytical to physical — narrow passages, scraping rock, reaching hands. The final section returns to interiority but the calculation engine is broken. Cole sits with the weight instead of running the numbers. This is the first chapter where Cole's math fails him not because the math was wrong, but because being right wasn't enough.

**Structural Notes:**
Four-beat structure: discovery (channels as system) → implication (colony's response, Elena's absence) → exploration (the confident descent) → catastrophe (Suri's death). The turn isn't a reversal — it's a revelation. Cole doesn't learn the planet is dangerous; he already knew that. He learns that understanding the danger doesn't protect you from it. The chapter is the longest sustained exploration sequence in the novel so far, building confidence deliberately so the collapse has further to fall.

**Continuity:**
- Count: 36 → 35 (Suri Park, mineralized creep activation in capillary channel, Month 12, Day 8)
- Suri Park: introduced Ch 4 (fruit incident), research assistant since, dies in channel exploration. Cole brings her. Cole's confidence kills her.
- Ancient channels: discovered Ch 12, Day 6. Now identified as planetary circulatory system. Deep channels fully mineralized (inert). Shallow/capillary channels incompletely mineralized (vulnerable to reactivation). Activation threshold tied to orbital cycle depth.
- Elena's logs: never mention channels. Her colony built in a different location (12km west). This is established as absence, not oversight.
- Settlement still unnamed — "Holdfast" reserved for Ch 23.
- Dana's creep-as-phase theory established here. Feeds directly into Ch 18 (creep solution using channels).
- James's compound fracture (Ch 12) not referenced — recovery assumed ongoing, will resurface in Ch 16.
- Patricia's cough (Ch 12) not referenced — will develop in Ch 16.

**Off-Voice Flags:**
The descent sequence risks becoming too cinematic — too much physical action, not enough interiority. Addressed by keeping Cole's internal calculation running throughout (distance measurements, compound behavior analysis, the math of the narrowing channel). Suri's death rendered through sensation and Cole's perception rather than graphic detail — consistent with Haruki's clinical economy in Ch 11. The final section's departure from Cole's calculation mode is intentional and should feel earned, not sentimental.
