## Craft Notes

- **Strongest asset:** Interiority. The deep POV is the novel's engine and it runs clean across all 27 chapters.
- **Structural discipline:** Every scene does at least two things. No exceptions found.
- **Elena parallel:** The ghost narrative parceled across Ch 6, 8, 10, 11, 17, 23 works beautifully as mirror and warning.
- **Dialogue:** Consistently excellent. "Said" and "asked" only. Action beats over tags throughout.
- **Closing line:** "That was something." — reverberation, not resolution. Exactly right.
- **Watch point:** The middle section (Ch 15-20) maintains a narrow emotional register during the grinding attrition phase. The pacing is intentional and structurally correct — the reader should feel the grind. But worth monitoring during revision for any passages where the narrowness tips into repetition rather than accumulation.
